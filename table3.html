The Old Legends of Karnataka Jnanapeeta award

There have been a Total of 8 Kannada writers from Karanataka who have received Jnanapeeta award.
These include:

  1. Kuvempu
  2. D R Bendre
  3. Shivaram Karanth
  4. Masti Venkatesha Iyengar
  5. V K Gokak
  6. U R Ananthamurthy
  7. Girish Karnad
  8. Chandrashekhara kambaara
